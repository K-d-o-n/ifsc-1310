What is Bouldering?

Bouldering: the art of climbing sick boulders that can be super short, or super tall. Bouldering is an extreme sub-sport of rock climbing that people can train for years and never be good at. You see, when it comes to boulding, skill is required to climb boulders that have intense movement that forces nearly every muscle within the human body to engage; mainly the brain. Bouldering can be quite dangerous at times, but it allows one to climb specific moves over and over until they're 100% dialed in, requiring near total perfection.
